This is a new way of communicating for the Gene Ontology, and we're not sure how well it will work.

In order to make the whole process go more smoothly, here are some etiquette suggestions for the webinar...
  1. Please keep your phone muted. The Gizmo Project teleconferencing software works well with many users as long as most users aren't transmitting data. If everyone keeps their phone except when asking a question, we should be fine.
  2. Use IRC to raise your hand. Before unmuting your phone, raise your hand in IRC and wait to be acknowledged. You can either raise your hand by typing "I have a question" (or something similar) or by typing "/me raises her hand". The second version will create a special message like "* Jane raises her hand"in the IRC room. I'll type "go ahead, Jane" in the IRC channel, and you can go ahead and ask your question.
  3. Test your configuration beforehand! Please make sure that all the tools work for you before we go into this seminar.  We are bound to have technical problems the first day, but it would be nice to have as few as possible.
